Chicken Schnitzel Recipe

Chicken Schnitzel

Chicken Schnitzel is a very delicious dish that originate from German, usually they use pork meat instead of chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1.5 tablespoons honey
  • 1.5 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoons and 0.75 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 0.5 tablespoon crushed garlic
  • 1.5 cups bread crumbs
  • 0.5 teaspoons salt
  • 0.5 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • vegetable oil
  • 6 boneless chicken breast
  • 0.5 lemon

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Whisk egg, dijon mustard, soy sauce, and crushed garlic together in a bowl. Mix bread crumbs, salt, and pepper together in a shallow bowl.

  2. Step 2

    3. Heat half of vegetable oil in a heavy skillet to 350 degrees F

  3. Step 3

    4. Put chicken breast in mixture and knead until fully coated. Press chicken in the bread crumbs until evenly coated

  4. Step 4

    5. Working in batches, carefully place chicken in hot oil using tongs and cook until no longer pink in the center and browned on the outside, about 3 to 4 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels and serve with lemon juice
