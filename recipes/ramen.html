Ramen Recipe

Ramen

Ramen is another one of common Japanese food, with their delicious soup from tonkotsu or pork stock amde them most favorite dish in Japan

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Kosher salt and ground black pepper
  • unsalted butter
  • sesame oil
  • fresh ginger (minced)
  • fresh garlic (minced)
  • Soy sauce
  • mirin
  • chicken stock
  • dried shiitake
  • large eggs
  • scallions (sliced)
  • ramen noodles

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Season chicken with kosher salt and pepper

  2. Step 2

    3. melt the butter in large skillet and add chicken cook until the color of chicken become golden brown

  3. Step 3

    4. Make the ramen soup, heat oil in large pot then add garlic, ginger and cook few minutes then add soy sauc, mirin stir to mix them then add the stock and let them boil, remove the lid and let simmer for 5 minutes then add the dried shiitake

  4. Step 4

    5. boil the egg, and in other pot also boil the noodle then serve
